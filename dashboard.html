How the risk score works
Each SKU's days-to-sell-out (current stock ÷ daily sales velocity) is compared against its days-to-expiry. When sell-out time exceeds expiry time, the item is flagged before it becomes a write-off — early enough to discount, push to high-intent users, or route to B2B/NGO partners instead of binning it. This v1 uses a deterministic formula; the next iteration plugs in a velocity model trained on hyperlocal demand, weather, and festival calendars.
