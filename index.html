Nearly 4x more SKUs per store in two years means far more perishable-item decisions per store, per day — more than manual FEFO checks can reliably catch.

The Solution

A risk score, not a postmortem

Instead of discovering expired stock during a manual check, Freshline flags it days in advance — early enough to act.

①

Per-SKU risk score

Compares days-to-sell-out (stock ÷ sales velocity) against days-to-expiry. Transparent formula — explainable and auditable, not a black box.

②

Automated actions

Graduated discounting, push notifications to nearby high-intent users, B2B offload, or NGO donation routing — before write-off, not after.

③

Store-level dashboard

One live, color-coded view per dark store, replacing manual FEFO checks across tens of thousands of SKUs.

④

Upgrade path to ML

V1 ships with a deterministic formula. V2 swaps in a velocity-prediction model trained on hyperlocal demand, weather, and festival calendars.
